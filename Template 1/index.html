About PRIMO

Primo is an initiative that brings critical thinkers together, to collaborate and build on innovative ideas and create an entity with ideals that seeks to supersede the restraints of companies or organisations, to create what will be the basis of a technological philosophy where innovation is for the every man and development is a never ending on going process.

An entity that is flexible enough to meet the needs and demands of its environment and not just monolithically develop a single idea or solve problems via a single approach, This philosophy aims to spark a new kind of technological revolution.

Primo will seek to cultivate creation and imagination without specialisation in any one particular field. we seek to be as broad as possible so as to tackle as wide a range of issues as possible , from Security to Transportation and from Consumer focused to State sponsored.

We have a very strong and enthusiastic team that is comprised of people from very diverse technical and non technical backgrounds who are ready to seamlessly and fluidly work together to achieve our goals.
